Dear User,

We do not like storing any personal or sensitive data and will therefore not ask you to make an account to use Project Kowalski. However, to keep all the data on our servers nicely organized, we ask you to set a session ID before starting using any of our tools. By attaching all your data to your session ID, we avoid any accidental data cross-over between users. Even though your data will be attached and saved under your session ID on our servers, we do not keep your data. Once you end your session or close your browser, your session ID and ALL corresponding data will be deleted on our end. Please make sure you download all the data you need before ending the session.
